About Ghitha

We're Providing The Best Solution

Ghitha Holding is an eminent trading and distribution firm operating as a retail subsidiary under IHC Abu Dhabi. Our specialization ranges from food to non-food produce and provides boundless services, including importing, trading, wrapping, storage, and more. We use advanced cold-storage equipment in our warehouses and logistic processes. Possessing over 10,000 products, we are proudly one of the top traders in the market while continuously evolving based on customer preferences. We supply quality products and food trading services by following HACCP and international standards of excellence for food safety and hygiene and have been accredited by ISO 9001:2015. Our products are imported from world-renowned manufacturers like the United States of America, Thailand, Brazil, Saudi Arabia, and Oman.

We prioritize time and quality every step of the way, to assure our customers of the best products.

International Holding Company (IHC) was founded in 1998 with a goal to diversify as a distributor and grow the non-oil business sectors in the UAE. In compliance with 'Abu Dhabi's Vision 2030', the ADX listed company aims to implement several initiatives such as sustainability, innovation and economic diversification across what is now considered as one of the region's largest restricted 2 conglomerates. IHC has a vision to enhance its' portfolio through acquisitions, business combinations and strategic investments. IHC is extremely proud to consist of 30 entities with future plans to diversify holdings across several growing industries such as Real Estate, Healthcare, Agriculture, Utlities, Food & Beverage, IT & Communications, Retail, Capital and Leisure.

IHC's primary focus is to enhance shareholder value and achieve significant growth in the coming years. Operational synergies and maximizing cost effectiveness across all verticals is where they thrive, furthermore, consistently evaluating investment opportunities through various means like direct ownership and securing partnerships in the UAE and internationally. While the world is changing every second, IHC focuses on resilience, innovation, and reshaping the marketplace for its own company, clients, and partners.

About Our Team

Ghitha Holding is an eminent trading and distribution firm operating as a retail subsidiary under IHC Abu Dhabi. Our specialization ranges from food to non-food produce and provides boundless services including importing, trading, wrapping, storage, and more. We use advanced cold-storage equipment in our warehouses and logistic processes. Possessing over 10,000 products, we are proudly one of the top traders in the market, while continuously evolving based on customer preferences. We supply quality products and services by following HACCP and international standards of excellence for food safety and hygiene and have been accredited by ISO 9001:2015.
